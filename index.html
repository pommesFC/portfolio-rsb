About

Hi, I'm Rohit Sharma Bhusal, a 20-year-old international student in Germany currently pursuing Studienkolleg (T-Kurs), a preparatory course that serves as a stepping stone towards my bachelor's degree. Originally from Nepal, My Studienkolleg experience, connecting with students from various backgrounds has expanded my worldview, fostering an appreciation for cultural diversity and a deep understanding of global perspectives.

This website is my dynamic playground—a space where I translate self-taught web development skills into practical expertise. Experimenting with HTML, CSS, and a touch of JavaScript, I turn theoretical knowledge into tangible creations.

Beyond academia, my interests span the worlds of football and cricket (I'm an avid FC Barcelona follower) and the enchantment of movies. Engaging in conversations is where I find joy, especially when delving into topics like physics, technology, existence, and purpose. Feel free to reach out—I'm open to discussions, eager to hear your views, and always up for learning something new.
